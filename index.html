About this Timeline

This is a timeline of 50 notable events in US history that changed the dynamic of American Federalism, through a constant push and pull of power between the federal government and the state and local governments. Each event is listed in chronological order on the left, with the title, date, and description. On the right is the relative amount of federal power up to that point in time. The amounts are cumulative; the dot will move a little futher to the right of the one above it if it increases federal power, and a little to the left if it decreases federal power. Enjoy this nice visual timeline!
